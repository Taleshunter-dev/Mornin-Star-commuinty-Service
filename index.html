Who we are?

At Morning Star Community Services, your care is our priority.

We are dedicated to providing person-centered NDIS support services designed to help participants live independently and achieve their life goals.


Our team works closely with participants and families to provide tailored services that align with their individual needs.

We believe in empowering individuals, supporting independence, and creating meaningful opportunities.

Vision Statement

To be a leading NDIS provider, where our participants are at the heart if everything we do, empowering them to live fulling and independent lives.

Mission Statement

To provide personalized support that prioritizes individuals' autonomy, dignity, and well-being, enabling them to make informed choices, pursue their passions, follow their dreams, and live a fun and fulfilling life

OUR VALUE STATEMENT

Why Choose Morning Star Community Services?

At Morning Star Community Services, we are committed to delivering high-quality, person-centred support hat empowers participants to live independently and achieve their goals. Our experienced and compassionate team works closely with participants, families, and stakeholders to ensure supports are tailored to each individual's needs. We pride ourselves on providing reliable services, maintaining the highest standards of care, and creating a supportive and inclusive environment where every participant feels valued and respected.

Respect

We respect each person’s choices, opinions, and perspectives, and value their right to make decisions about their own life.
